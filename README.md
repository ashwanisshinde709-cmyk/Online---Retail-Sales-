# Online---Retail-Sales-
Excel bases retail sales analysis project with cleaned data, pivot tables, dashboard, insights and business recommendations.

## 📌 Overall Conclusion

- 💰 The business has a **strong overall performance** with:
  - **$10.6M** in total revenue
  - **19,960** invoices
  - Operations across **38 countries**

- 📈 Sales peak during the **September–November** period, indicating strong seasonal demand.

- ⚠️ Revenue is **highly dependent on the UK market**, creating a concentration risk.

- 🚀 Recommendations:
  - Expand into international markets to reduce dependency on the UK.
  - Diversify the product catalog to attract a wider customer base.
  - Improve inventory planning to meet seasonal demand efficiently.
