# 📊 Online Retail Sales Analysis (Excel Dashboard Project)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an **Excel-based sales analysis** of an online retail dataset. The objective was to transform raw transaction data into meaningful business insights using Microsoft Excel. The project includes data cleaning, data transformation, pivot table analysis, an interactive dashboard, and business recommendations.

The analysis was completed entirely in **Microsoft Excel** using **Power Query**, **Pivot Tables**, **Pivot Charts**, **Slicers**, **Timeline**, and dashboard design techniques.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The goal of this project is to:

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Identify revenue trends over time.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Compare domestic and international sales.
- Discover top-performing products and countries.
- Present insights through an interactive dashboard.
- Provide business recommendations based on data.

---

# 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains online retail transaction records, including:

- Invoice Number
- Invoice Date
- Customer ID
- Product Description
- Quantity
- Unit Price
- Country
- Revenue (Calculated)

The data was cleaned before analysis by handling missing values, removing duplicate records where appropriate, correcting data types, and preparing the dataset for reporting.

# 📌 Key Business Insights

### 💰 Strong Overall Business Performance

- The business generated **$10.6M** in revenue.
- Nearly **20,000 invoices** indicate a high transaction volume.
- Sales were recorded across **38 countries**, demonstrating an international customer base.

---

### 📈 Seasonal Sales Trend

- Sales increased significantly between **September and November**.
- This period represents the strongest sales season and may be influenced by holiday shopping or seasonal demand.

---

### 🌍 Geographic Performance

- Revenue is heavily concentrated in the domestic market.
- International markets contribute a smaller portion of overall sales, indicating opportunities for future expansion.

---

### 📦 Product Concentration

- A small group of products contributes a large share of total revenue.
- Monitoring inventory and availability of these products is important for maintaining sales performance.

---

### 💳 Customer Purchasing Pattern

- Invoice analysis helps identify purchasing behavior and average spending.
- Customer-level analysis supports future marketing and customer segmentation strategies.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Expand marketing efforts in international markets to diversify revenue sources.
- Maintain sufficient inventory during the September–November peak sales period.
- Reduce dependence on a small number of high-performing products by promoting additional product categories.
- Continue monitoring customer purchasing trends to improve retention and increase repeat sales.
- Use dashboard insights for regular business performance reviews and decision-making.

---

# ⚠️ Data Notes

- The dataset for **December 2011** may be incomplete.
- Any decline in sales during this period should **not** be interpreted as a true decrease in business performance without confirming data completeness.
